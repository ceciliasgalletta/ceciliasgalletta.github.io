TriesteNEXT

TriesteNEXT is an annual festival dedicated to fostering innovation, promoting scientific research, and encouraging public engagement with cutting-edge ideas. Held in Trieste, the event brings together researchers and the general public to explore the latest advancements in science and technology through talks, exhibitions, workshops, and interactive activities.

I had the privilege of discussing the computational challenges of my pulsar evolution research with Physics of Data students at the University of Padova, set in the beautiful surroundings of Venice.
